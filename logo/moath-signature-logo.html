How to use it

  • Header logo on the site — pair it with the "moath.dev" wordmark in the nav, or use it alone for a more confident look.
  • Favicon — works clean at 20×20 thanks to the increased stroke weight. Browser tab icon, bookmark, PWA icon all covered.
  • Social media avatars — works on LinkedIn, GitHub, Twitter, X. The warm variant on dark stands out best in feeds.
  • Email signature — small inline version below your name. Same one you sign on paper, now in pixels.
  • Business cards / CV — top-left corner of your CV, business card, or invoice. Quietly authoritative.
  • It's already a signature — this is the rarest thing in design: a brand that's genuinely yours. Nobody else has this mark.
